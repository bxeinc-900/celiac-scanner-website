**📱 3 Simple Steps to Safety**

**Step 1: Point and Scan the Ingredients**Traditional barcode scanners often rely on outdated databases**. Celiac Scanner is different: it uses ****Gemini 3 Multimodal AI** to actually "read" the physical ingredient list right on the package**. Because it reads the text directly, it easily identifies hidden gluten sources like Malt, Brewer's Yeast, and Yeast Extract that often hide behind generic barcodes**.

**Step 2: Instant Expert Cross-Referencing**The second you scan a label, the app goes to work. It triggers a real-time cross-reference against our **"Trust 30" Database Consensus**. Your product is instantly checked against more than 30 authoritative medical sources—including the Celiac Disease Foundation, Mayo Clinic, and Columbia University Celiac Center**. You don't have to guess; you get a verdict backed by the world's leading experts**.

**Step 3: Get Your Traffic Light Verdict**We know you are busy, so we keep things incredibly simple for when you are standing in a crowded grocery aisle**. You will instantly receive a color-coded alert:**

* **🟢 ****SAFE (Green):** Confirmed safe by the ingredients and official medical databases**.**
* **🟡 ****CAUTION (Amber):** The app flags ambiguous ingredients (like "Spices") and suggests you check the package for an official GF certification**.**
* **🔴 ****GLUTEN FOUND (Red):** An immediate alert that identifies the specific prohibited grain or gluten derivative**.**
